# !financials Sector Financial Edge Cases

This is command-local support for !financials. It does not replace GLOBAL.md, SOURCES.md, METRICS.md, OUTPUT.md, or ARTIFACTS.md.

Use these compact checks when a sector's financial statements can mislead a simple revenue/margin/FCF read.

## Brokerage / custody fintech

- Check segregated cash, user payables, securities borrowed/loaned, clearing deposits, receivables from users/brokers/dealers, and other client-balance-sheet movements.
- CFO distortion can make filing-based FCF look stronger or weaker than recurring owner earnings.
- Revenue can be activity-sensitive: options, equities, crypto, event contracts, net interest, securities lending, subscriptions, routing/PFOF, or partner concentration.
- If buybacks are used to offset SBC, show both repurchase activity and share-count/SBC trends when material.

## Banks / financial platforms

- Separate corporate liquidity from customer/client/trust/secured-facility balances.
- For lending, card, custody, clearing, or receivables activity, identify credit quality, funding source, recourse, capital requirements, and balance-sheet growth.
- Gross margin may be unavailable or not meaningful for financial platforms; do not force software-style margin analysis.

## Software / SaaS cash conversion

- Review deferred revenue, billings, RPO, retention/churn, customer concentration, SBC, capitalized software, and sales efficiency when disclosed.
- CFO/FCF strength may depend on upfront billing, working capital, or deferred-revenue growth; caveat cash conversion when relevant.
- Treat repurchase authorization as dilution mitigation only after checking actual repurchases and share-count/SBC trends.

## Capital-intensive infrastructure

- Capex, depreciation, leases, convertibles, asset impairments, dilution, RPO/backlog/contract value, and project timing can dominate financial quality.
- Direct or segment gross margin may exclude depreciation/amortization and may not prove full-cycle economics.
- Do not treat headline contract value as RPO unless the filing says it is included, enforceable, delivered/accepted, or otherwise recognized under the revenue policy.
- Compare operating cash flow with capex and financing needs to assess whether growth is self-funding.

## Commodity / miners

- Separate reported revenue growth from realized price, production volume, grade/yield, cost inflation, sustaining capex, depletion, reclamation, and working-capital swings.
- Digital assets, inventory-like commodities, hedges, fair-value remeasurement, collateralized debt, and cycle exposure can distort liquidity and earnings quality.
- Cash-only liquidity may matter when commodity/digital-asset holdings dominate broader liquid assets.

## Semiconductors / hardware / acquisition accounting

- Watch inventory, customer concentration, channel inventory, purchase accounting, amortization, restructuring, impairments, warranty exposure, and cyclicality.
- Earnings releases may present GAAP gross margin differently from statement-line cost of revenue when acquired-intangible amortization is handled outside cost of revenue.
- Do not mix statement-line and release-presented margin bases without labeling the basis.

## Equity-funded growth

- Make share count, warrants, converts, ATM programs, registered directs, PIPEs, preferred stock, earnouts, SBC, and post-period issuance visible when material.
- A large cash balance can be real and useful, but if it came from equity issuance while CFO/FCF remains negative, label liquidity strength separately from internally generated financial strength.
