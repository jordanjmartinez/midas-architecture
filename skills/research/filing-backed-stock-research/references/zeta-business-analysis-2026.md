- Do not claim strong pricing power unless directly supported. The filings disclose intense competition, price cutting, downward gross-margin pressure, and customer price sensitivity. Source area: 2025 10-K lines 1665-1669.
- Balance positive spend-expansion signals against constraints: super-scaled ARPU growth and cross-sell/module expansion are positives, but usage-based revenue, non-automatic renewal in many relationships, customer concentration, and competitive pressure limit certainty.
